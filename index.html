About Me

Where Heritage
Meets Fashion

Born and raised with deep roots in Indian culture, I bring the richness of South Asian aesthetics to every frame. From traditional bridal couture to modern fusion wear, my work celebrates the beauty of ethnic fashion on the global stage.

Based in Houston, Texas, I specialize in ethnic and bridal fashion editorials, runway shows, and commercial campaigns that honor the artistry of Indian craftsmanship. Whether it's the intricate zardozi of a lehenga or the flowing drape of a Kanchipuram silk, I believe every garment tells a story.
